## Understanding the Basics
When tackling Data Cabling, many homeowners underestimate the complexity of modern systems. Whether you are dealing with solid stone walls in a heritage property or trying to achieve gigabit speeds across a large estate, standard consumer-grade equipment often fails to deliver consistent results.

### Common Pitfalls
1.  **Inferior Cabling:** Using CCA (Copper Clad Aluminium) instead of solid copper leads to signal degradation and POE failures.
2.  **Poor Placement:** Incorrect node positioning in mesh systems creates "bottleneck" zones.
3.  **Lack of Shielding:** External runs without proper UV-rated shielding will fail within 24 months.

## Technical Specifications & Best Practices
